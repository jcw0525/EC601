classroom language learning tool via google streetview simulated immersion in navigation

teacher creates a scenario for classroom exercise: a sequence of streetview panoramas with a point of interest described by landmark-based navigation instructions in foreign language
student parses directions for each panorama and clicks on sequence of points of interests until arrives at final destination - score is a combination of total time minus misclicks

ex.
- french teacher creates a sequence of streetview panoramas in paris - from a hotel to the effiel tower - defined by a list of instructions ("take a left at the river" or "go towards the cinema")
- teacher shares exrercise with students via link
- each student goes through a "where's waldo" for the described point of interest in each panorama
